Giving Back To The Society

Swachh Bharat - Clean India

I am a proud intern of the NSS Swachh Bharat Summer Internship-Government of India, a fantastic program of India's Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports. Sithalapakkam, a hamlet in the Kancheepuram district of Tamil Nadu, India, became our adopted village. The internship did not provide a salary because it was a volunteer activity done for social good.

During this time, we (my team and I) had to focus on the critical aspects of sanitation and hygiene in our assigned community. We picked a school and began raising awareness about the importance of cleanliness and hygiene for ourselves and society. For 30 days, we cleaned the schools, streets, and nearly every other aspect of that community.

We educated the people on solid waste management. We taught them how to separate biodegradable and non-biodegradable trash. Days went by, those 30 days sped by, and it was time to leave. Giving back to society is extremely rewarding and beneficial. “Service to others is the rent you pay for your room here on Earth” a wise person once stated.
